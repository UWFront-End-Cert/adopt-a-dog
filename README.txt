Adoptadog is an e-commerce website for the adoption of dogs in the Greater Seattle Area.

Pages include:
  - home page
    - preview of a handful of Dogs
    - preview of blog posts
  - dog profile page
    - full selection of dogs available for adoption
  - blog page
    - each blog post contains an image and a post. new blog posts should be appended closest to the top
  - checkout
    - image and name of dogs, price
    - form

Basic style guide included in adoptadog-testing.txt
